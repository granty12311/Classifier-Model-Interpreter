================================================================================
                              COLUMN DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

LOAN IDENTIFICATION & AMOUNTS:
-------------------------------
1.  id                    - Unique loan identifier
2.  member_id            - Unique member/borrower identifier  
3.  loan_amnt            - The listed amount of the loan applied for
4.  funded_amnt          - The total amount committed to that loan
5.  funded_amnt_inv      - The total amount committed by investors for that loan
6.  term                 - The number of payments on the loan (36 or 60 months)

INTEREST & PAYMENT:
-------------------
7.  int_rate             - Interest rate on the loan
8.  installment          - The monthly payment owed if the loan originates
9.  grade                - LC assigned loan grade (A-G)
10. sub_grade            - LC assigned loan subgrade (A1-G5)

BORROWER INFORMATION:
---------------------
11. emp_title            - Job title supplied by the borrower
12. emp_length           - Employment length in years (<1 year to 10+ years)
13. home_ownership       - Home ownership status (RENT, OWN, MORTGAGE, etc.)
14. annual_inc           - Self-reported annual income
15. verification_status  - Status of income verification (Verified, Not Verified, Source Verified)

LOAN DETAILS:
-------------
16. issue_d              - The month the loan was funded
17. loan_status          - Current status of the loan (Fully Paid, Current, Charged Off, etc.)
18. pymnt_plan           - Indicates if a payment plan is in place (y/n)
19. url                  - URL for the LC page with listing data
20. desc                 - Loan description provided by the borrower
21. purpose              - Category provided by borrower for loan request
22. title                - Loan title provided by the borrower
23. zip_code             - First 3 numbers of zip code
24. addr_state           - State provided by borrower

CREDIT HISTORY:
---------------
25. dti                  - Debt-to-income ratio
26. delinq_2yrs          - Number of 30+ days delinquencies in past 2 years
27. earliest_cr_line     - Month the borrower's earliest credit line was opened
28. inq_last_6mths       - Number of inquiries in past 6 months
29. mths_since_last_delinq - Months since last delinquency
30. mths_since_last_record - Months since last public record

ACCOUNT INFORMATION:
--------------------
31. open_acc             - Number of open credit lines
32. pub_rec              - Number of derogatory public records
33. revol_bal            - Total credit revolving balance
34. revol_util           - Revolving line utilization rate
35. total_acc            - Total number of credit lines

LOAN PERFORMANCE:
-----------------
36. initial_list_status  - Initial listing status (w=whole, f=fractional)
37. out_prncp            - Remaining outstanding principal for total amount funded
38. out_prncp_inv        - Remaining outstanding principal for investors
39. total_pymnt          - Payments received to date for total amount funded
40. total_pymnt_inv      - Payments received to date for investors
41. total_rec_prncp      - Principal received to date
42. total_rec_int        - Interest received to date
43. total_rec_late_fee   - Late fees received to date
44. recoveries           - Post charge off gross recovery
45. collection_recovery_fee - Post charge off collection fee

PAYMENT TRACKING:
-----------------
46. last_pymnt_d         - Last month payment was received
47. last_pymnt_amnt      - Last total payment amount received
48. next_pymnt_d         - Next scheduled payment date
49. last_credit_pull_d   - Most recent month LC pulled credit

ADDITIONAL METRICS:
-------------------
50. collections_12_mths_ex_med - Number of collections in 12 months excluding medical
51. mths_since_last_major_derog - Months since most recent 90-day or worse rating
52. policy_code          - Publicly available policy code
53. application_type     - Individual or joint application
54. annual_inc_joint     - Combined self-reported annual income (joint applications)
55. dti_joint            - Combined debt-to-income ratio (joint applications)
56. verification_status_joint - Income verification status for co-borrower
57. acc_now_delinq       - Number of accounts currently delinquent
58. tot_coll_amt         - Total collection amounts ever owed
59. tot_cur_bal          - Total current balance of all accounts

RECENT ACCOUNT ACTIVITY:
-------------------------
60. open_acc_6m          - Number of open trades in last 6 months
61. open_il_6m           - Number of installment accounts opened in past 6 months
62. open_il_12m          - Number of installment accounts opened in past 12 months
63. open_il_24m          - Number of installment accounts opened in past 24 months
64. mths_since_rcnt_il   - Months since most recent installment accounts opened
65. total_bal_il         - Total current balance of all installment accounts
66. il_util              - Ratio of total balance to high credit/credit limit on installment accounts
67. open_rv_12m          - Number of revolving trades opened in past 12 months
68. open_rv_24m          - Number of revolving trades opened in past 24 months
69. max_bal_bc           - Maximum current balance owed on all revolving accounts
70. all_util             - Balance to credit limit on all trades
71. total_rev_hi_lim     - Total revolving high credit/credit limit

INQUIRIES:
----------
72. inq_fi               - Number of personal finance inquiries
73. total_cu_tl          - Number of finance trades
74. inq_last_12m         - Number of credit inquiries in past 12 months

================================================================================
                              DATA CHARACTERISTICS
================================================================================

LOAN AMOUNTS:
-------------
- Range from small personal loans ($2,400) to larger amounts ($10,000+)
- Funded amounts may differ from requested amounts

LOAN TERMS:
-----------
- Two options: 36 months (3 years) or 60 months (5 years)

INTEREST RATES:
---------------
- Vary based on grade (7.9% to 21.28% in sample)
- Higher grades (A) have lower rates, lower grades (F) have higher rates

LOAN GRADES:
------------
- Letter grades from A to G
- Sub-grades provide more granularity (A1-A5, B1-B5, etc.)

LOAN PURPOSES:
--------------
Common categories include:
- credit_card (credit card refinancing)
- debt_consolidation
- small_business
- car
- wedding
- other (with specific descriptions)

LOAN STATUS:
------------
- Fully Paid: Loan successfully paid off
- Current: Loan is active and payments up to date
- Charged Off: Loan defaulted and charged off
- Various other statuses for different stages

GEOGRAPHIC DISTRIBUTION:
------------------------
- Covers multiple US states
- Zip codes partially masked for privacy (only first 3 digits)

EMPLOYMENT:
-----------
- Employment length ranges from <1 year to 10+ years
- Various employers and job titles included

VERIFICATION:
-------------
- Income can be: Not Verified, Source Verified, or Verified
- Different levels of verification affect loan terms

================================================================================
                                    NOTES
================================================================================

1. This is a LendingClub loan dataset containing peer-to-peer lending information
2. Dataset appears to span from 2011-2016 based on date columns
3. Contains both successful (Fully Paid) and unsuccessful (Charged Off) loans
4. Rich feature set for credit risk modeling and analysis
5. Personal information is partially anonymized (zip codes, URLs)
6. Many columns have missing values (indicated by empty fields in CSV)
7. Currency values are in USD
8. Dates are in Mon-YYYY format
